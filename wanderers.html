MIGHTY MUKURU WANDERES

Exclusive News

Mighty Mukru wandered exclusive news for the 2024 season

1. Exciting New Signings for 2024

Mighty Mukuru Wanderers have strengthened their squad by signing talented players, including striker Maxwell Gunda from Blue Eagles and midfielder John Banda from UD Songo (Mozambique).

2. Return of a Legend

Former Wanderers star Joseph Kamwendo has rejoined the club as part of the technical team, taking up the role of assistant coach.

3. New Sponsorship Deal

Wanderers have renewed their partnership with Mukuru for the 2024 season, with an increased sponsorship deal worth MWK 500 million to boost player welfare and infrastructure.

4. Focus on CAF Competitions

The team was committed to prioritizing CAF Confederation Cup matches, with hopes of progressing beyond the preliminary stages for the first time in three years.

5. Leadership Changes

The club has appointed former player Clement Kafwafwa as the new Team Manager, bringing a wealth of e xperience to the role.

Additional News

Top Scorer Target:

Team captain Stanley Sanudi has set a personal goal of scoring 15 goals this season while also aiming to lead the team to domestic glory.

Exclusive New Kit Launch:

Wanderers revealed their 2024 kit featuring a striking blue-and-white design inspired by their rich history.

Player Milestones:

Yamikani Chester is set to celebrate his 100th appearance for the club this season.

Injury Comebacks:

Key players Peter Cholopi and Vitumbiko Kumwenda have returned to full training after missing most of the 2023 season due to injuries.

Fan Engagement Events:

Wanderers will hosted a "Meet the Players" event in March 2024 to enhance fan interaction, including autographs, photo sessions, and merchandise giveaways.
