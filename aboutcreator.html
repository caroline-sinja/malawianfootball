About the Creator

Hi, I'm Caroline Sinja!

I’m an 18-year-old, first-year student at the Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences (MUBAS). I created this website as a personal project to bring together my passion for football and Web Development.

As a fan of Malawi's local football teams like Mighty Mukuru Wanderers, I wanted to create a space where fans could stay up-to-date on the 2024 football season. This project also allows me to apply what I’m learning in school and develop my web development skills.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy exploring the website!
