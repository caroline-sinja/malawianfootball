NEWS

Moyale Barracks FC, based in Mzuzu, has had a competitive 2024 season in the Malawi TNM Super League. The team showed resilience and improvement throughout the year, securing key victories and holding their ground against strong opponents. Some highlights of their season include a 2-0 victory against Karonga United and a hard-fought 2-1 win over FOMO FC. They also managed draws against notable teams like Silver Strikers and FCB Nyasa Big Bullets, reflecting their defensive solidity. However, they faced challenges in matches against Mighty Mukuru Wanderers and other top-tier teams. The club has been consistent in its performance at the Mzuzu Stadium, where their fans have shown unwavering support. Moyale Barracks has also actively participated in fostering young talent, emphasizing long-term growth and development in the sport​​​​​​.
