NEWS

Fomo FC, based in Mulanje, had exciting plans for 2024 as they join the TNM Super League for the first time in their history. After winning the 2023 ThumbsUp Championship, the team secured promotion through a dramatic penalty shootout against Ntopwa FC. Their entry into the Super League marks a significant milestone, and they aim to establish themselves among Malawi's elite teams​​​​.

KEY UPDATES AND ACTIVITIES

1. Participation in the Castel Challenge Cup: Fomo FC recently progressed to the national stage of this competition, showing resilience by defeating Mwanza Stars in a tense penalty shootout. They are now set to face Super League teams, showcasing their determination to excel on a national level​​.

2. Coaching Changes: The team recently appointed Gilbert Chirwa, a former Malawi national team player and CAF Class A license holder, as their head coach. His focus is on blending experienced players with existing talent to build a competitive team. This strategic leadership change is expected to drive better results and foster a winning mentality​​.

3. Team Preparation: With ambitions to compete strongly in both the Super League and cup competitions, Fomo FC is emphasizing teamwork, rigorous preparation, and community support. The management is also assembling a strong backroom staff to bolster their efforts​​​​.

** These developments highlight Fomo FC’s dedication to making their mark in the 2024 season, with strong performances in league and cup fixtures as their main goals.
