Here are details about Chitipa United’s scheduled activities and performance in 2024:

The team has experienced mixed results, showing strong performances against teams like Karonga United but struggling against top clubs like Big Bullets.

Chitipa United had exciting lineup of activities during the 2024 season, starting with pre season preparation. In january, the team held a two-weeks training camp in Karonga focusing on fitness, team bonding and drill to ensure readiness for the 2024 season.

Despite Chitipa United's struggles throughout the seaon, they secured significant victories, showcasing their determination to remain competitive among Malawi's top football clubs. One of their key achievements was the hard-fought 1 - 0 victory against FCB Nyasa Big Bullets which is one of the league's strongest team.

Additionally, Chitipa United climbed to the top of the league standings mid-season after a 2 - 0 win against Bangwe All Stars. Goals from Lorenzo Ghedo and Godfrey Kwiyokwa, enhanced the team's dominance in the match and the teams goal GoalKeeper George Chikooka made critical saves to maintain their clean sheet.

While the team faced tough defeats, such as 2 - 0 loss to silver-strikers, their ability to recover and adapt was evident. The team's defensive organisation often kept their matches competitive even against stronger opponents​​​​
