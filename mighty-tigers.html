The Mighty Tigers, known for their determination and passion, are set to make waves in the 2024 Malawi football season. As a team with a history of resilience, they continue to strive for success in every match they play.

NEWS

The Mighty Tigers FC, based in Blantyre, Malawi, participates in the TNM Super League and other domestic competitions. Here’s an overview of their 2024 activities:

The Mighty Tigers competed in various league fixtures, facing top teams like Big Bullets, Mighty Mukuru Wanderers, Silver Strikers, and others throughout the season. These matches are critical for their league standings.

Mighty Tigers had an eventiful start to the season. They faced a touch match against Creck SC loosing 3 - 1 in Their opening game. But their win against Bangwe All Stars boosted their standing and kept their hopes in the league. Despite struggling with some inconsistent performance, Tigers remained competitive, particularlyin their matches against top teams like FCB Nyasa Big Bullets.

Mighty Tigers is Known for producing talented players, the club continues its commitment to nurturing talent, contributing to the broader football ecosystem in Malawi​​​​​​.
