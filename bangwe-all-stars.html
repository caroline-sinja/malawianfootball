TNM SUPER LEAGUE

Bangwe All stars aimed to solodify their position as one of the Malawi's footbal team's The club planed to focus on improving its league standing compared to the previuos season. The team earned signifiant victory against Nyasa Big Bullets with a 1 - 0 win. The goal came at 71st munute with Clever Chikwata. This victory marked a turning point for Bangwe All Stars, showcasing their resilience and stategic game play.

Despite their triumphs, the team faced challenges against stronger teams such as Mighty Wanderers wo defeteated them 1 - 0 in an earlier match. The teams performance this season highlighted mixed forms, as they battle to improve their league standing.

LEAGUE STANDING

As of their last matches, Bangwe sat in 13th place, battling to avoid relegation​​.
