KAMUZU-BARRACKS-FC

Kamuzu Barracks, a team built on resilience and hard work, was ready for an exciting 2024 season. With a strong focus on strategy and teamwork, they were determined to achieve success on the pitch.

NEWS

Kamuzu Barracks FC had an active 2024 season competing in the TNM Super League and various domestic cups. The team performed well in several matches, such as a 2-0 victory over Dedza Dynamos, with goals from Christopher Mtambo and Gregory Nachipo. Despite their competitive efforts, they were eliminated in the semifinals of the Airtel Top 8 by Nyasa Big Bullets in a challenging match that ended 2-0, showcasing their strong defense but struggling offensively against tougher opponents​​​​​​.

In the league, Kamuzu Barracks maintained mid-table stability, ranking 5th during critical stages of the season. Their gameplay often demonstrated disciplined defensive structures and occasional moments of offensive brilliance, yet consistency in scoring was a challenge. The team displayed resilience, adapting tactics against top teams like Nyasa Big Bullets and Dedza Dynamos​​​​.
