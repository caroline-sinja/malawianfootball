PRE-SEASON PREPERATION

Training Camp

The team held its pre-season training camp in January 2024 at the Kamuzu Institute for Sports. Emphasis was on fitness, tactical drills, and team bonding.

Another pivotal moment was their battle with Nyasa Big Bullets in the FDH Bank Cup. Although the team fell short, their defense and tactical play were praised, with players exhibiting exceptional teamwork and determination throughout the match. These games highlighted their potential and ability to compete against top-tier teams​​​​.
