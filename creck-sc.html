CRECK-SPORTING-CLUB

The dynamic CRECK SC which has just been promoted to play in the TNM super league was set to mark the 2024 season with determination, skill and to climb the standings and secure a strong position in this season. Follow the team's 2024 updates, matches, results and squad.

NEWS

Creck SC has experienced a dynamic 2024 season in the Malawi Super League. The team showcased its best performance on August 10 with an emphatic 8-0 victory over Baka City, a match that highlighted their attacking prowess and tactical dominance. Another notable success was their 2-0 win against Dedza Dynamos on August 1, demonstrating their ability to secure vital home victories. Additionally, they edged out Bangwe All Stars with a narrow 1-0 win on July 13, underlining their defensive resilience​​​​.

Despite these wins, Creck SC faced challenges throughout the season. They endured tough losses, including a 3-2 defeat to Big Bullets on September 25 and a 3-0 loss to Mighty Wanderers on April 20. However, they managed some solid draws, such as a 1-1 match against the league leaders, Big Bullets, on June 23, and a 2-2 result against Kamuzu Barracks on July 24, which demonstrated their ability to compete against strong opponents​​​​.

As the season progresses, Creck SC continues to aim for a stronger league position, preparing for upcoming matches and building on their mixed results. The team's focus remains on maintaining consistency and climbing the standings​​​​.
