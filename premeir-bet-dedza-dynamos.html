PREMEIR-BET-DEDZA-DYNAMOS

The energetic Premier Bet Dedza Dynamos entered the 2024 season with high hopes and a drive to succeed.The team is known for their dynamic style, they aimed to bring excitement to every match.

NEWS

Premier Bet Dedza Dynamos has an eventful 2024 season marked by sponsorship milestones and competitive challenges. In April 2024, the club secured a significant MK100 million sponsorship deal from Premier Bet Malawi, replacing their previous sponsor. This deal introduced initiatives such as awards for Player of the Month and Fan of the Year, emphasizing both player performance and fan engagement​​.

The team is actively competing in the TNM Super League and other tournaments like the Airtel Top 8. Despite a challenging 4-1 defeat to Silver Strikers in the first leg of the Airtel Top 8 quarterfinals, Dedza Dynamos is determined to improve its performance in upcoming matches. Their coach has expressed optimism and commitment to overcoming setbacks despite missing key players due to injuries​​​​.

The team faced challenges in maintaining consistency but has managed some standout performances, particularly at home.
