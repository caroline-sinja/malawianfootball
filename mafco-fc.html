Mafco FC, also known as the Malawi Armed Forces College Football Club, has been actively competing in various competitions in 2024, including the Malawi Super League, League Cup, Challenge Cup, and Top 8 Cup. Here are some of the notable activities and results:

Super League Performance

Mafco has experienced a mixed season, achieving notable wins like a 2-1 victory over the Mighty Tigers on July 13 and a 1-0 win against FOMO FC on August 11. Some of their matches ended in draws, such as against Civil Service United (0-0 on September 18) and Mighty Wanderers (0-0 on July 21). The team has faced challenging defeats, including losses to FCB Nyasa Big Bullets (1-0 on October 6) and Dedza Dynamos (3-0 on May 5)​​​​.
