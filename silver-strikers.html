SILVER-STRIKERS

Whether a thrilling home game or a challenging away fixture, the Silver Strikers never fail to impress. With a mix of experienced leaders and young talents, they poised to be serious contenders for the league title. Keep up with their latest fixtures, results, and squad updates.

NEWS

AIRTEL TOP 8 CUP

Silver Strikers had an impressive run in the Airtel Top 8, securing a 4-1 victory over Dedza Dynamos in the quarterfinals. This commanding win gave them a significant advantage going into the second leg​​​​. They progressed to the semifinals, showcasing their tactical discipline and high pressing game. The team strived to reach the finals and claim the trophy. Unfoutunatley they lost to Nyasa Big Bullets by a score of 1 - 0. The decisive goal came in the final minutes of the match, leading to disappointment to the team's head coach Peter Mponda. Mponda highlighted that it is just the loss of luck not skill!!

SUPER LEAGUE

The team maintained their strong form in the Super League, securing the 1st posiion. Silver Strikers' coach, Peter Mponda, praised the team's resilience throughout the season, particularly as they kept their momentum even in challenging matches​​.

FISD CHALLENGE CUP

In a tightly contested final of the FISD Challenge Cup, Silver Strikers faced Be Forward Wanderers. The match ended in a 3-2 defeat, but the team displayed strong performances despite the loss​​.

ADDITIONAL

Silver Strikers' 2024 season was marked by resilience and competitive spirit across all major tournaments. Despite facing tough competition, including defeats in the Airtel Top 8 semifinals and the FISD Challenge Cup final, they remained one of the top contenders in Malawian football, showcasing both tactical discipline and attacking strength.
